I'm a user experience designer.*

I specialize in asking questions. I try to ask the right questions to the right people at the right time. To get to an appropriate solution, you must determine the right problem to solve. 

*"User experience" encompasses all aspects of the end user's interaction with the company, its services, and its products.1

Collaborative Development

In my work experience, I have always worked closely with software developers. I have a thorough understanding of web development and can communicate with various teams easily.

I have found that close communication with development throughout the ideation process can lead to faster delivery of working increments. 

Standards and Documentation 

I have a weakness for committing to standards when it comes to the use of a design tool, a project, department operations, or pretty much anything at work. If there are existing standards in place, I'll adopt them and provide ongoing feedback. If there aren't... well, I'll want to write something up.
