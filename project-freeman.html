Freeman Audio Visual Canada

March 2016 - October 2016 (CONTRACT)

Freeman is a global leader in brand experience marketing. Freeman Audio Visual is Canada's leader in audio visual solutions and event production.

At Freeman Audio Visual, I was entrusted to lead the contrent migration from an outdated website to a modern and accessible content management system (CMS).

Collaborative Development

This project was completed in collaboration with Scott Newlands, who was hired by Freeman to lead the design and development of the Wordpress CMS.

An additional statement.

My Role

  • I coordinated the migration of content by prioritizing and assessing the site map to determine a new information architecture
  • I collaborated with subject matter experts throughout the organization to create and curate photos, videos and written content
  • I led the development of an internal and external social media strategy to share content from event producers across Canada
